what we're about

As kids in the Comox Valley, Peter and George ignited their passion for architecture through playful adventures with LEGO bricks. From those early building blocks, a lifelong fascination grew, shaping them into visionary architects.

United by the joy of creation, their childhood LEGO escapades became the foundation for a shared commitment to crafting imaginative and extraordinary structures that now define their architectural journey.

Former colleagues turned friends, Peter and George converged in the heart of the Comox Valley, founding Perspective Design Build Ltd. Their shared passion for design and construction fueled the company's inception.

From humble beginnings, the duo's unwavering commitment to excellence and client satisfaction propelled their venture into a thriving design-build firm. Together, they've built a community of wonderful clients turned friends, transforming dreams into architectural masterpieces.
