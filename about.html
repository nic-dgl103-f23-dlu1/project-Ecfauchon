meet our team

George Slomp

George has been building since he was a boy on a farm in Rimbey Alberta, building barns and making foundations. He funded his way through University framing houses and pouring concrete. In 2011 George, his wife and 3 children moved to Courtenay, BC. He eventually started his own company, Steadfast Carpentry. Peter and George merged their two companies in 2016 and became Perspective Design Build and havn't looked back. George works hard and is easy to talk to. He is the guy who you'll hear whistling while he works.

Peter Bain

Peter is passionate about creating beautiful and functional design. Born in Haida Gwaii, Peter spent much of his childhood observing and learning from nature and from his father - a forester. He studied mechanical engineering at BCIT and humanities at Langara College in Vancouver. During his First 10 years of Carpentry, he built architectural homes with a West Vancouver builder and in 2011 completed his red seal in Carpentry. Comox Valley is now home, where Peter has embraced his finishing and cabinetry skills as PeterBain Carpentry, In the last 4 years a partnership with George Slomp has formed as Perspective Design LTD. Peter works well with his clients in a creative process with attentive listening, problem solving, and patience - all of which are key attributes in building quality custom homes.
